About Manav Seva Sanstha

A decade of excellence in transforming lives through skill development, women & youth empowerment, and sustainable community growth across India

Who We Are

Pioneering Change Since 2014

Manav Seva Sanstha is an ISO 9001:2015 certified non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through innovative skill development, education, healthcare, and women & youth empowerment initiatives.

As a pan-India training partner with NSDC and Skill India ministry, we have transformed over 15,000 lives through vocational training and provided 3,000+ job placements across various sectors including IT-ITES, Beauty & Wellness, Automotive, and Technical trades.

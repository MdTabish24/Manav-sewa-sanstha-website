Welcome to Manav Seva Sanstha

Manav Seva Sanstha is an ISO 9001:2015 certified non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities through skill development, education, healthcare, and women empowerment initiatives. Founded a decade ago and registered under Maharashtra Public Trust Act 1950, we are committed to creating sustainable livelihoods and fostering inclusive growth across India.

As a pan-India training partner with NSDC (National Skill Development Corporation) and Skill India ministry, we have transformed over 15,000 lives through vocational training and provided 3,000+ job placements in various sectors.
